What is DaGoose?

DaGoose was founded by Goose in April 2020 during the COVID-19 lockdowns. Initially, DaGoose was a YouTube channel dedicated to open-source software tutorials. More recently, we have developed our own open-source software and printed circuit board designs and released them publicly free of charge.

SMT-PBTS v3.0

An open source, Hi-Fi Bluetooth speaker printed circuit board designed for the smallest, best sounding portable Bluetooth speaker applications. About the size of a credit card, it features multi-channel configurations for an immersive soundstage, a programmable audio DSP, Bluetooth v5.0 technology with external antenna support, 24-bit HI-RES audio support, and more.
